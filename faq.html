FAQS

  1. Where does PantherTech meet?
    • We meet at the very far back of the band hallway in Western High School.
  2. How much does it cost to join?
    • Team fees are 200$ per year. This includes hotel stay, food, and other fees. If paying the 200$ is an issue, let a mentor know. We try our best to accommodate for students.
  3. When are the meetings?
    • Off Season: Thursdays 6-8PM
    • Build Season: Monday-Thursday 6-8PM. Saturday 10AM-4PM
    • Please note, sometimes meetings have to be rescheduled or canceled. The most update to date info can be found in the team's discord.
  4. What events does the team go to?
    • The team goes to 2 types of events. On-season and Off-season.
    • For Off-season typically the team attends Boiler Bot Battle in October.
    • For On-Season we always go to 2 district events, but we must qualify for State and Worlds events.
    • To see what events we are attending, visit First Inspires
